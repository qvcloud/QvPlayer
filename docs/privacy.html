QvPlayer Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

QvPlayer (hereinafter referred to as "the App") values your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect any personally identifiable information. QvPlayer is a local player tool. All playlists, video URLs, and configuration information are stored locally on your device (Apple TV).

2. Local Network Access

The App requests local network access for the following purposes only:

  • To provide a built-in Web Management Interface, allowing you to manage playlists from a computer browser.
  • To discover and play media resources within your local network.

3. Third-Party Services

The App integrates open-source libraries such as KSPlayer and FFmpeg for video decoding. These libraries run locally and do not send your data to third parties.

4. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally, the security of your data depends on the security of your device. We recommend protecting your local network environment.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the support page within the App.
